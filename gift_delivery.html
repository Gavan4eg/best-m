Gift delivery
You can send special gifts, perfume, fruit baskets, 3 or more roses, teddy bears or other toys, a bottle of sparkling wine, chocolates and a card to your lady or friend who is in Ukraine and they will receive it within a few days!
Proof of prompt delivery will be sending you several photos of the lady/friends with gifts or flowers at the time of delivery.
There is NO FEE for delivery!

Guarantee:

We guarantee fresh flowers and quality service. If not or we are unable to deliver the goods to the recipient, we will refund 100% of the money.

(If for seasonal and regional reasons we have to change the type of flowers, we will inform the customer.
If we do not receive a response in time, we will replace the flowers with flowers of the same type and at the same price.)
