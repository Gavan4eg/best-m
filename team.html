History

Best-Matchmaking was founded over a decade ago as a local dating agency. But we quickly realized that the market is teeming with different online dating agencies rendering non-personalized services, so we have to stand out of all of them to connect more and more single people willing to meet each other.

And a few years later, although we already have created tens of happy international couples, we decided to stop being a drop in the ocean of the dating agencies and become a unique dating and matchmaking service with their personalized and unique approach which no other agency or site can offer.

So here we are – an American matchmaking company with over 30 representatives in all Ukraine, Latvia, and Russia. Our team is absolutely unique. Each of our managers has his/her unique and rich experience in the dating and matchmaking field. They have hundreds of happy couples under their belts!
